DOCUMENT 1: Initial Police Report

On January 15, 2024, at approximately 2:30 PM, officers responded to a domestic disturbance call at 123 Main Street. The complainant, Mrs. Sarah Johnson, reported that her husband, Mr. Michael Johnson, had become verbally abusive during an argument about finances.

Mrs. Johnson stated that Mr. Johnson threw a vase against the wall, breaking it into several pieces. She also mentioned that this was not the first incident of this nature. When asked about previous incidents, she mentioned two prior occasions where similar behavior occurred.

Mr. Johnson was not present at the scene when officers arrived. Mrs. Johnson provided a description of her husband: male, 45 years old, 6'2" tall, brown hair, wearing a blue shirt and jeans.

Officers observed broken glass on the floor consistent with a vase being thrown. No injuries were reported. Mrs. Johnson declined medical attention.

The incident was classified as domestic abuse. Mr. Johnson will be contacted for his version of events.
