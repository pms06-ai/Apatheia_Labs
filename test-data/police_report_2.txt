DOCUMENT 2: Follow-up Police Report

On January 16, 2024, officers followed up on the domestic disturbance call from the previous day. Mrs. Sarah Johnson was interviewed again.

Mrs. Johnson clarified that the incident occurred on January 14, 2024, not January 15 as initially reported. She stated that Mr. Michael Johnson had not thrown any objects, but had only raised his voice during the argument.

When questioned about the broken vase mentioned in the initial report, Mrs. Johnson stated that she had accidentally knocked it off a shelf herself during the argument. She also mentioned that there had been only one previous incident, not two as she had initially stated.

Mr. Johnson was located and interviewed. He denied any abusive behavior and stated that Mrs. Johnson had been drinking and became aggressive towards him. He described himself as male, 42 years old, 5'10" tall, blonde hair.

Officers noted that Mrs. Johnson appeared nervous during the interview. No further action was taken pending additional information.
