## Phase 1: ANCHOR - Origin Point Identification

### Primary ANCHOR Document: E002 (Claire Francis, 17 Sept 2021)

This document establishes foundational claims that propagate unchanged through subsequent assessments.

#### Origin Claims Established:

**CLAIM 1: Father Lacks Autism Understanding**
> "Mr Dunmore does not fully appreciate Ryan's diagnosis of Autism" [E002]

- **Origin Context:** Mother's account during telephone interview
- **Verification:** None documented
- **Propagation:** Repeated in E006, F032 without independent verification

**CLAIM 2: Parenting Style Concerns**
> "There were clearly some concerns raised in 2017, these were not deemed sufficiently serious" [E002]

- **Origin Context:** Maternal grandfather raised concerns about bruising
- **Verification:** "Police welfare check raised no concerns regarding physical abuse"
- **Critical Omission:** Pattern of MGF hostility not flagged

**CLAIM 3: Father's Alienation Concerns Minimized**
> "Ryan has been told his father does not care about him" [E002:107]

- **Status:** Documented but not weighted equally with mother's concerns

#### CRITICAL CONTEXT DOCUMENTED (LATER STRIPPED):

**COVID Hospitalization of Baby Freya:**
> "Freya became very ill and had to be hospitalized due to Covid-19 complications" [E002]

This is the ONLY document to record this significant family trauma. It establishes:
- July 2021 contact breakdown had medical context
- Baby sister's hospitalization created genuine family crisis
- Father's infection preceded chain transmission

**This context is ABSENT from all subsequent CAFCASS documents.**

## Phase 3: COMPOUND - Authority Accumulation

### Document F032 (Lucy Walton, Section 7 Report, Completed 8 March 2023)

**CRITICAL TIMING:** Completed 21 days before murders (29 March 2023)

This document represents maximum authority accumulation - a formal Section 7 Report under FPR 16.20.

#### Inherited Claims Now Treated as Established Fact:

**"No Safeguarding Concerns" - Threshold Finding:**
> "There are no current safeguarding concerns that would suggest that Ryan is not safe in the care of his father" [F032:222]

This claim has now appeared in:
1. E002 (Sept 2021): "No safeguarding concerns identified that would prevent Ryan spending time with his father"
2. E006 (Nov 2022): "Issues raised by parents during interviews would not prevent Ryan from spending time with either parent"
3. F032 (Mar 2023): "No current safeguarding concerns"

**COMPOUNDING PATTERN:** Same conclusion, different words, no new investigation.

#### Mother Characterization Escalation:

| Document | Characterization |
|----------|-----------------|
| E002 | Neutral - both parents have concerns about other |
| E006 | "Mrs Stephen shared that Ryan... often presents himself as anxious" |
| F032 | **"Ms Stephen does not recognise the value of Ryan's relationship with his father and extended family"** [F032:242] |

**Directional Shift:** Progressive negative characterization of mother across 18 months.

#### School Observation Weaponized:

> "Ryan 'parrots' what he hears - on weekends with mother, positive about moving; on weekends with father, talks about missing him" [F032:69]

- **Framing:** Implies maternal influence over Ryan's expressed views
- **Alternative Interpretation:** Child expressing context-appropriate views to each parent
- **Application:** Used to discount Ryan's expressed preferences for mother

#### August 2022 Physical Allegation - Final Treatment:

> "Ryan disclosed being punched by his father (August 2022)" [F032:141]
> "Play fights with Ryan but does not hit him - 'punched' disclosure not accurate" [F032:161]

- **Resolution:** Father's denial accepted
- **Investigation:** None documented
- **Finding:** Subsumed into "no safeguarding concerns"

## Phase 4: ARRIVE - Outcome Mapping

### The CASCADE Failure Pattern

**Pre-Murder State (8 March 2023):**
- Four CAFCASS professionals across 18 months
- "No safeguarding concerns" - universal finding
- Mother characterized as not recognizing father's relationship value
- Ryan recommended to remain in jurisdiction with current arrangements

**Post-Murder Reality (29 March 2023):**
- Maternal grandparents commit double murder
- Ryan present in house during/after killings
- CAFCASS "no safeguarding concerns" finding now archived

### Critical Questions for Regulatory Complaint:

1. **COVID Context Stripping:** Why was baby Freya's hospitalization - documented in E002 - absent from all subsequent assessments? This context explained the July 2021 contact breakdown.

2. **Circular Validation:** Each subsequent CAFCASS professional cited "concerns raised and addressed in previous proceedings" without independent verification. Is this compliant with CAFCASS Operating Framework?

3. **Maternal Grandfather Pattern:** 2017 referrals came from maternal grandfather. This same individual would commit murder in 2023. Pattern of MGF hostility to father visible across documents but never connected.

4. **Physical Allegation Closure:** August 2022 "punched" disclosure raised in E006, dismissed in F032. What investigation occurred? Where is documentation?

5. **Threshold Finding Propagation:** "No safeguarding concerns" inherited across four assessments. Did any professional conduct fresh risk assessment or rely on inherited conclusions?
